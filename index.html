About Me

I am excited to begin this journey into a brand new career path. In the past, I've been a "Jill of all trades" providing everything from business analysis, executive support, meeting and event planning, IT recruiting, and procurement. No career path has harnessed my desire to creatively and logically solve problems like web development. I can't wait to join a team and provide clean, innovative solutions for client problems.

As an avid reader, I've completed 26 books this year alone, averaging one a week. I also love to attempt to cook - the fire department isn't on speed dial yet!
